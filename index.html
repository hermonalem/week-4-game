Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below and by clicking on a crystal you will add specific amount of points to your total score. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until click on it. You win the game by matching your score to the random number, you lose the game if your score exceeds the random number. The random number and the value of the crystals change at the start of each game.
